Setting up and managing your account on [10101.art](https://10101.art) is a straightforward process. 
Follow these steps to ensure your profile is secure and ready to engage with our platform.

## Pre-Setup

Before you begin, you'll need to set up a digital wallet to interact with our platform. We recommend using [MetaMask](https://metamask.io/), a secure and popular wallet for managing your assets. Alternatively, you can use other wallets supported by [WalletConnect](https://walletconnect.com/).


<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="Install MetaMask">

<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
    Visit the [MetaMask website](https://metamask.io/) and download the extension for your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Brave, or Edge) or download the MetaMask app for your mobile device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Second Step">
    Follow the installation instructions and add the extension to your browser or install the app on your phone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Third Step">
    Open MetaMask and click on **Get Started**. <br />
    Select **Create a Wallet** and follow the prompts to set up a new wallet.
  </Step>
</Steps>


### Important
**Save your seed phrase** (a series of 12 or 24 words) in a secure location. <br />
**This phrase is the only way to recover your wallet** if you forget your password or lose access to your device. <br />
**Do not share your seed phrase with anyone**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Use WalletConnect">

<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
    If you prefer using a different wallet, make sure it supports [WalletConnect](https://walletconnect.com/).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Second Step">
    Follow the instructions specific to your wallet for setup and connection to [10101.art](https://10101.art).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Third Step">
    Ensure you securely store your seed phrase for recovery purposes.
  </Step>
</Steps>
  </Accordion>
  </AccordionGroup>


## Creating an Account


<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
    Click on **Connect Wallet**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Second Step">
    Choose your option **MetaMask** or **WalletConnect**.
  </Step>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="If you select MetaMask">
  <Step title="Third Step">
    - A window will appear prompting you to sign a transaction for login.
    - Agree to the platform's [Terms of Service](https://10101.art/files/terms&conditions.pdf) and confirm you are at least 13 years old.
  </Step>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="If you select WalletConnect">
    - A window will appear prompting you to select your wallet.
    - Scan the QR code with your mobile wallet or connect your hardware wallet to the device.
    - Sign the transaction to proceed.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

  <Step title="Fourth Step">
    After signing the transaction, you will be logged into your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>
