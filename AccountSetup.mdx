Setting up and managing your account on [10101.art](https://10101.art) is a straightforward process. 
Follow these steps to ensure your profile is secure and ready to engage with our platform.

## Pre-Setup

Before you begin, you'll need to set up a digital wallet to interact with our platform. We recommend using [MetaMask](https://metamask.io/), a secure and popular wallet for managing your assets. Alternatively, you can use other wallets supported by [WalletConnect](https://walletconnect.com/).



  <Accordion title="Install MetaMask">

<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
    Visit the [MetaMask website](https://metamask.io/) and download the extension for your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Brave, or Edge) or download the MetaMask app for your mobile device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Second Step">
    Follow the installation instructions and add the extension to your browser or install the app on your phone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Third Step">
    Open MetaMask and click on **Get Started**
    Select **Create a Wallet** and follow the prompts to set up a new wallet.
  </Step>
</Steps>


**Important**: Save your seed phrase (a series of 12 or 24 words) in a secure location. **This phrase is the only way to recover your wallet** if you forget your password or lose access to your device. </br >**Do not share your seed phrase with anyone**.
  </Accordion>
