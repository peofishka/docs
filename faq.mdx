# General Questions


<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is 10101 Marketplace?">
10101 Marketplace is a platform that bridges traditional art with the digital era by offering co-ownership of real-world art assets through blockchain technology and NFTs.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is NFT from 10101.art?">
It is a blockchain certificate that confirms co-ownership of the physical painting by those who hold it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What blockchain do we use?">
Currently our platform operates on Ethereum blockchain. But we also plan to launch some collections using other blockchains, like Polygon, in the near future.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does it work?">
<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
Our artexperts select exclusive paintings from top collectors, auction houses, and galleries. Check all the documents for the authenticity of the work of art.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Second Step">
Next, the projectâ€™s top management decides whether the painting will be presented on 10101.art. If so, we organize the transportation of the painting to Dubai. Create digital representations of it using high-end scanning equipment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Third Step">
Our skilled developers digitalize the artwork fragments into NFT-tokens. Launch the primary sale using NFT technology, with or without a whitelist.
  </Step>
   <Step title="Third Step">
With 100% sale of the painting within the time limit, NFTs become available for further transactions.
  </Step>
</Steps>


*If a painting is not completely sold within the allotted time, all issued NFTs are burned, and the refund option becomes available to users.*
  </Accordion>

 <Accordion title="What does an NFT owner get?">
	10101.art NFTs give each user legally verified co-ownership of a physical artwork with a confirmed value. <br /><br />

	Any user can now enter a totally new market, collect, trade and grant pieces of paintings. Top up, change and modify their portfolio.<br /><br />

	All the paintings sold at the platform are presented in Monada Art gallery in DIFC, Dubai and available for public viewing.<br /><br />

	In the near future DAO mechanics will be implemented and owners will be able to vote for the destiny of the painting. Whether to exhibit it in specific countries, sell it offline, burn its physical embodiment.<br /><br />
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How does 10101 Marketplace ensure the authenticity of artworks?">
	Each artwork is rigorously verified for its origin and legitimacy, with all records securely stored on the blockchain to prevent fraud and ensure transparency.
</Accordion>
