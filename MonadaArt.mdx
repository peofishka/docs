# 10101.art x Monada Art Gallery

At [10101.art](https://10101.art), we’re reshaping the way people think about art ownership through the innovative process of RWA (real-world asset) tokenization. This cutting-edge method **allows more people to own a piece of cultural history** by converting physical art into digital pieces. **No longer limited** to the wealthy elite, access to the works of art by the greatest artists like Picasso, Dali, or Banksy is **now democratized**.

**Our mission** is built on the foundation of blockchain technology, ensuring that **every transaction is authentic, secure, and transparent**. This technological backbone not only makes fine art more accessible but also revolutionizes the way we interact with and appreciate art, fostering a global community of art collectors..


To provide a tangible connection to the masterpieces on our platform, all artworks are displayed at the [Monada Art Gallery](https://monada-art.gallery/). This partnership ensures that **every piece can be viewed and appreciated in person**, adding a layer of physical accessibility to our digital innovation.

**[Located](https://maps.app.goo.gl/AEAhUseLEvLW5CJA6)** in the bustling heart of Dubai’s financial district, **Monada Art Gallery** is even more than just a place to display art. **Monada Art Gallery** is the representative of the innovative area where the future and the past exist in symbiosis giving birth to the present. The gallery’s dedication to revitalizing the appreciation of fine art aligns perfectly with our mission at [10101.art](https://10101.art) to democratize art ownership through technology.
